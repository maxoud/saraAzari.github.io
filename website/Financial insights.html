Financial insights

Financial insights brings real time visibility into how a SNF is performing in terms of PDPM revenue. Performance information is available at mutiple levels, including organizational, facility, and resident level.

Why

Problem definition and use case

CMS (centers for medicare and medicaid services) introduced PDPM (patient driven payment model) and SNFs (Senior nursing facilities) needed to make changes and adapt. PDPM revolutionized how SNFs operate by changing the way SNFs get paid and ensuring coordinated, quality care becomes a reality.
Upcoming changes in the processes concerned Pointclickcare customers, and product managers spotted the need to provide the customers with a real-time, dashboard-like solution that could give SNF administrators quick and ready to consume insights on how their facilities are doing.

Who

Target persona for Financial Insights is Adam, the administrator.


The process

User research

PDPM was a new concept to everyone, and users naturally did not use this product before. This means all the user research prior to release had to be done with users that had no idea what they were expecting from this product. This made user testing to be a little challenging. Customers were still excited that an analytics product was on its way to help them make decisions fast and easy, and without having to look at multiple excel pages and studying numbers.
During bi-weekly remote feedback sessions with 4 customers prior to the first release, decisions about the direction the product was going were made. Some of the most helpful pieces of feedback include

  • Learning based on which KPIs administrators make decisions. This is important because we wanted cater the information presented to users without taking away any value
  • Dashboard content: Users helped us narrow down and prioritize the number of KPIs that we included on the “overview page” of the application. This is important because we wanted this page to give users the most valuable insight, as fast as possible
